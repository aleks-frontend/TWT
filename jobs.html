  • UX and Interaction Designer

    Are you interested in working on a cutting-edge network operation center? The Production Infrastructure Engineering team (PIE) is growing to further delight Microsoft’s customers in a Cloud First world by delivering highly available online services and cutting-edge features with innovative functionality.z

    You'll design experiences that solve complex interactions with elegance and simplicity. The role’s broad scope will give you ample room to flex your design muscles—both on projects that have an immediate impact on the Azure business through explorations that shape our strategy, vision, and UX direction.

    Requirements & responsibilities:

    • 3+ years’ experience in UX design, Interaction design, and/or Service design, across academia and/or industry (REQUIRED)
    • Ability to create highly polished design prototypes, mockups, and other communication artifacts
    • Ability to quickly turn around rigorous wireframes and mockups quickly in a fast-paced, iterative environment
    • Ability to scope and estimate efforts accurately, prioritize tasks and goals independently, and rapidly produce deliverables
    • Give and solicit feedback from designers and cross-disciplinary partners
    • Proficiency in a variety of design tools such as Illustrator, XD, Sketch, Figmaz
